Poker Hands

Features
- Simple simulation of poker with deck creation, shuffling, etc
- Display card images to drawn cards
- Find exact hand (flush, full house, etc)
- Allows running 100 and 10000 times very efficiently
- Efficient unbiased random algorithm to shuffle cards (Fisher Yates shuffle)
- Displays statistics

Card images: http://www.waste.org/~oxymoron/cards/
Poker hand information: https://www.pokerstars.com/pages/poker/hand-rankings/
